Restaurant Interview

By Wilse Ramos

A man decided that he canted to apply to be a dishwasher in his local restaurant, and so he called in to get a interview which he was and so he was told to come the next week and he decides to train by washing more dishes than ever before, cooking even more and doing more cleaning around the house, but just before he thinks that hes ready he must face off against other competition that also want the spot just like him, what will he do?. And fast foward the week goes by and so he get's ready and proceed to go out and hope to get the interview which we will see just how that goes for him...
